# 📐 Irregular Solid Volume Calculator

A simple and user‑friendly web‑based calculator designed to compute the total volume of the **Tapered Dome Prism** — an irregular solid composed of three different geometric shapes.

---

## 📌 About the Project
This tool was developed to make volume calculations easier, faster, and more accurate. Instead of solving manually step‑by‑step, you just need to enter the measurements, and the calculator will instantly give you the volume of each part and the total volume.

### 🧱 Structure of the Solid
The Tapered Dome Prism is made up of:
1. **Rectangular Prism** – serves as the base
2. **Cylinder** – forms the middle section
3. **Hemisphere** – creates the rounded dome at the top

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used
- **HTML** – for the structure and content
- **CSS** – for styling and minimalist light pink design
- **JavaScript** – for the calculation logic and interactivity

---

## 🚀 How to Use
1. Enter the required measurements for each part:
   - Length, Width, and Height of the Rectangular Prism
   - Radius and Height of the Cylinder
   - Radius of the Hemisphere
2. Click the **Calculate Total Volume** button
3. View the results: individual volumes and the total volume will be displayed below

---

## 📝 Formulas Used
| Shape               | Formula                                  |
|---------------------|------------------------------------------|
| Rectangular Prism   | \( V = l \times w \times h \)            |
| Cylinder            | \( V = \pi r^2 h \)                      |
| Hemisphere          | \( V = \frac{2}{3} \pi r^3 \)            |
| **Total Volume**    | \( V_{total} = V_{prism} + V_{cylinder} + V_{hemisphere} \) |
